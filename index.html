Stages of creating a website

1 Plan & Concept

  • Define Goals: Identify what you want visitors to do (e.g., contact, buy, subscribe).
  • Target Audience: Pinpoint who you're trying to reach (e.g., demographics, interests).
  • Competitor Research: Analyze top competitors, find strengths & weaknesses.
  • Content Strategy: Plan content types (text, images, video) for each page.

2 Design & Develop

  • Platform Selection: Choose website builder (Wix), CMS (WordPress), or custom build.
  • UI/UX Design: Craft a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.
  • Responsiveness: Ensure smooth functionality across all devices (desktop, mobile).
  • Features & Integrations: Add functionalities (e-commerce) and integrate relevant tools.

3 Test & Launch

  • Thorough Testing: For bugs, broken links, functionality across browsers and devices.
  • User Testing: Get feedback on website navigation and design from target audience.
  • Launch Preparation: Secure web hosting, register domain name, and optimize SEO.
  • Go Live & Promote: Launch website and advertise through marketing channels.

4 Maintain & Grow

  • Analytics Monitoring: Track website traffic, user behavior, and conversions.
  • Data-Driven Optimization: Use data to identify areas for improvement and optimize.
  • Security Maintenance: Update software & plugins, implement strong security measures.
  • Adaptation: Update content regularly, adapt to user feedback and industry trends.
